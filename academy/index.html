The Bamboo Principle

The Root Season

The Chinese bamboo tree takes five years to break through the ground. For those five years, it must be watered and weeded every single day.

If you stop for just one day, the tree dies in the ground.

But then, in the fifth year, it grows 80 feet in just six weeks. Did it grow in six weeks or five years? It grew in five years. If it hadn't grown deep roots, it could never sustain that height.
